Projects
10th F1Tenth Autonomous Grand Prix (ICRA 2022, Philadelphia)
Archit Hardikar, Lenning Davis, Nicholas Marion, Ruchi Gupte,
Summer 2022

In this project, our team participated in the 10th Autonomous Racing F1Tenth competition held at IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation 2022, and successfully secured the title as the second fastest lap time. Our autonomous racing car used the 25ms fast update from the Hokuyu 2D LIDAR and used an advanced spline based RRT* algorithm coupled with Pure Pursuit to plan a path around the opponent. Localization was done using Hector SLAM package and the particle filter was tuned to make the localization more accurate for head-to-head racing.

Iterative Close Point (ICP) Scan Match Algorithm
